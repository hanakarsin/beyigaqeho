    Reactions As Ganduje Rides Kwankwaso On Motorcycle In Epic Throwback Photo

    A throwback photo of immediate past governor of Kano, Umar Ganduje giving a ride to the presidential candidate of the New Nigeria People Party (NNPP) in the 2023 general elections, Rabiu Kwankwaso on a motorcycle has surfaced on social media.

    31 Jul 2024, 00:00

    A throwback photo of immediate past governor of Kano, Umar Ganduje giving a ride to the presidential candidate of the New Nigeria People Party (NNPP) in the 2023 general elections, Rabiu Kwankwaso on a motorcycle has surfaced on social media.

    Naija News understands that Ganduje and Kwankwaso over recent years have become like cat and dog.

    Speaking on their feud, George Udom shared the photo on Facebook with the caption, “Let us not allow politics to destroy our hard earned relationships. This is Ganduje giving ride to Kwankwaso on a Vesper in 1981.”

    The photo since it surfaced social media have sparked reactions from Nigerians.

    Izonborn wrote: ‘Change is constant.The stronger the friendship/relationship the bitter the enemity.’

    Merch wrote: ‘We were once close friends, we became strangers when we started making money-meek mill.’

    Agbadocassava wrote; ‘People don’t know that Ganduje daughter married Kwakwanso’s son.’

    Jumper524 wrote: “This is why Ganduje has refused to become kwankwaso puppet. Kwankwaso problem with Ganduje started when he left APC and Ganduje his mate in politics refused to.Ganduje resilience showed us that he’s not a. Second class citizen to kwankwaso as it is presumed which was clearly visible in the last kano governorship election. Despite the widespread and red cap movement of kwankwaso, it was a tough race for both parties.”
