    'Claim to Fame': Lark's Identity Is Finally Revealed on the ABC Competition Show!

    By Pretty Honore

    Sep. 6 2022, Updated 11:54 a.m. ET

    Spoiler alert: This article contains spoilers for Claim to Fame.

    ABC’s hit reality show Claim to Fame features 12 contestants who are related to a celebrity and attempt to solve their fellow housemates' identities by putting together clues they receive throughout the competition. Each week, one contestant is voted as the "guesser" and has to pick another player and guess their identity.

    As the season has progressed, viewers have been eager to uncover the cast’s secrets. Brittany, Amara, and Adria disclosed the identities of their celebrity relatives early on, but there are a few cast members that have tried to throw off their competitors — one of which is Lark.

    While fans put together clues as to which celebrity Lark is related to, her fellow housemates were seemingly stumped. However, in Episode 9, L.C. was pretty confident in her theory. So, was she right? Keep reading to find out more about who Lark from Claim to Fame really is.

    Lark on 'Claim to Fame' — The Clues

    Throughout the series, we discovered two very telling clues about the Lark. First, the reality TV star revealed how she's related to her mystery family member. Lark shared that her celebrity relative is her aunt. Later, we also learned that the woman in question was a world-class supermodel in the '80s.

    Lark on 'Claim to Fame' — The Guesses

    Claim to Fame cast members have to put in some serious detective work to win and take home the $100,000 grand prize. However, without access to the outside world, their resources are limited.

    Claim to Fame star Pepper had a strong theory that Lark is related to Martha Stewart, which Lark played to her strengths and let her housemates think.

    However, in Episode 9, L.C. put together all of Lark's clues and felt confident in her theory. Going against her gut, Pepper was voted as the guesser and took L.C.'s advice, correctly guessing Lark's celebrity identity.

    So, who is Lark on 'Claim to Fame’?

    Lark (born Lark Skov) is related to Cindy Crawford, which is something fans on Twitter seemed to figure out a while ago. One fan wrote, “Lark is 100 percent related to Cindy Crawford! Kaia Gerber, her daughter, follows Lark on Instagram.”

    Prior to Lark’s identity being officially revealed, social media sleuths confirmed her identity. A 2014 article published by the Daily Mail features a picture of Lark and names her as Cindy’s niece. In the photo, Lark is dressed up as her aunt for Halloween.

    Lark hails from Colorado Springs, Colo., and graduated from the University of Washington — where she rose to popularity as an esteemed steamboat rower — in 2020.

    Catch new episodes of Claim to Fame Mondays at 10 p.m. EST on ABC, or on Hulu.
