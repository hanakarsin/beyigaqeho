    Nick Groff is an American paranormal investigator and musician famous for the television series “Paranormal Lockdown”. Groff has also served as co-investigator and executive producer on Ghost Adventures. His line of work is quite similar to that of Zak Bagans, who also calls himself a ghost hunter.

    Aside from his work on Paranormal Lockdown, Nick has been working on many different projects. Despite his involvement in various productions, Groff has leaned primarily towards the paranormal. In addition, Groff has also collaborated with channels such as TLC and Destination America. Now let’s get to know a little more about Groff, shall we?

    Nick Groff: early life and education

    Nicholas Groff was born on April 19, 1980 in San Jose, California. Despite being born in California, Groff grew up in Nashua, New Hampshire. He had a passion for the supernatural from a very young age. He was a very adventurous boy and was eager to explore places. However, due to his passion, he had a near death experience when he was 8 years old. Groff was playing and climbing a tree and suddenly fell from a high branch.

    Now looking at his academics, Nick went to Pelham High School in New Hampshire. After graduating from high school, Groff joined the University of Nevada, graduating with a degree in film.

    Nick Groff: career

    Nick met Zak Bagans in 2004 and surprisingly they both had an unusual interest in the paranormal. Although they began their careers as a DJ, they knew they had to come together to investigate the paranormal. With that in mind, Nick and Zak hired Aaron Goodwin and included him on their team. It was the beginning of a new adventure for them. In 2004, the trio began visiting some of the scariest places in Nevada looking for any paranormal activity. This interesting subject made a show called Ghost Adventures. Additionally, Nick has also worked for the 2012 TV show Nightline.

    A couple of years later, in 2014, Nick started working as an investigator for Ghost Adventures: Aftershocks. He has also contributed to Ghosts of Shepherdstown. In more recent years, Nick has been cast as the lead investigator for the show, Paranormal Lockdown.

    Nick Groff: personal life

    Now looking at the personal aspects of Groffs life, he has been married to Veronique Roussel. The two met in high school and Groff gave Roussel her ring in her senior year. The two then said their vows in 2004 and Zak Bagans DJed the ceremony.

    Nick and Veronique have been together for a long time. The couple currently lives in New England and has given birth to two wonderful children. Their first daughter Annabelle was born in 2010 and Chloe in 2014.

    Nick Groff: Net Worth and Social Media

    Nick has been involved in the entertainment industry for many years. He began his career in music and later established himself as one of the leading players in paranormal investigations. Although Groff is still dedicated to music, his main focus remains on television. With everything he does, Nick has been able to earn a good sum of money. Reports suggest that Groff has a net worth of $1 million.

    Groff is also quite active on social media platforms. You can find him on Instagram where he goes for the handle @nickgroff_ and has over 204,000 followers.
