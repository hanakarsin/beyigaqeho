    Well, as it appears, some of our Tanzanian neighbours have studied and understood the Kikuyu language and can sing in it fantastically.

    This was evident when Tanzania's gospel choir Zabron met Kenyan gospel star Evelyn Wanjiru who has been on tour ministering to our neighbours.

    In a video initially shared on her Instagram but since gone viral, the incredible choir surrounded Evelyn. They then sang their famous song Mkono Wa Bwana, but now in a Kikuyu rendition.

    Like seasoned Kikuyu speakers, the choir could be seen hitting the notes with a little difference in the accent, which would be expected.

    The Zabron singers really impressed Evelyn, who was filled with joy as she lauded the team for the incredible rendition while speaking in Kikuyu herself.

    In the end, she made a hilarious joke alerting River Road for their arrival, a place not well known for its good side.

    In her caption, the gospel star celebrated the group praising them while also urging them to keep serving God.
