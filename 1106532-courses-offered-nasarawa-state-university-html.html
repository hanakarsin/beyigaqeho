    Admission requirements overview

    Yahaya outlines the general admission requirements for NSUK, highlighting that they follow a standard five-subject criterion, depending on the program or course a candidate wishes to pursue. He said:

    Typically, candidates must have credits in Mathematics and English at their O-level. The other three subjects depend on the particular course they are applying for.

    Course-specific requirements

    Each course at NSUK has specific subject requirements that candidates must meet. Prospective students can visit the university's website for detailed course information and subject specifications. This resource lists the courses you must sit for during your Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) Unified Tertiary Matriculation Exam (UTME) and each course's required O-level subject combinations.

    To illustrate, Yahaya provides an example from the university's Faculty of Administration, which includes courses like accounting, banking and finance, business administration, entrepreneurship, public administration, marketing, taxation, and security and investment studies.     Common challenges and advice

    Yahaya also addresses a common issue faced by prospective students. He mentioned:

    Nasarawa State University admission requirements

    For you to gain admission to Nasarawa State University, there are certain requirements that every student should meet. They include:

    • NSUK must be your top choice university during the UTME application process.
    • Choose NSUK as the first-choice institution and score 140 and above.
    • Attain a minimum of 5 credits, including English and Mathematics, in a maximum of 2 sittings.
    • The credit passes must include English Language and Mathematics.
    • All UTME candidates must participate in the NSUK post-UTME screening exercise.
    • Candidates must have an 'A' Level/IJMB pass in two subjects related to their desired program of study.
    • When offered admission, candidates must accept or reject the admission on CAPS.

    How can you apply for Nasarawa State University admission?

    To get admission, you have to apply through the university's admission portal. Follow the steps below to apply.

  • Visit the official Nasarawa State University portal.
  • Click on "Portal" and then click next to the link written "Not yet a student or a returning applicant?"
  • Create an account by selecting your application type, email address and password.
  • Verify your email and log in using your user ID and password.
  • Proceed with the application by filling out the online application form and providing accurate and complete information.
  • Scan and upload relevant documents like certificates, exam results, and reference letters.
  • Make the required payment through the designated channels.
  • Review your application thoroughly before final submission.
