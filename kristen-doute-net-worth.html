    Kristen Doute's Net Worth Is as Big as Her Personality

    Kristen Doute has worked hard for her net worth. She has multiple business ventures and has even written a book.

    By Sheridan Singleton

    May 9 2024, Published 8:17 a.m. ET

    The premier season of The Valley has been a rollercoaster ride in the best way possible. The drama is at a high, and it's all thanks to one very important agent of chaos, Kristen Doute. The time away from Vanderpump Rules has changed Kristen some, but her penchant for sharing info at the most inopportune, or some would say opportune moment, hasn't changed. Now she has a staunch supporter by her side and boyfriend, Luke Broderick, who has stood by her as the group dynamics have started to crumble.

    Kristen kicked things off in Episode 2, accusing Janet Caperna of saying that Michelle Lall was a racist and a Republican. This came out while Janet wasn't present, leading to a lot of she said, he said, she said, and she said. This tragic game of telephone led to more distrust within the group against Kristen, while also masking the offensive comment Michelle allegedly made. Regardless of the drama, Kristen is doing just fine on her own. So, what is Kristen Doute's net worth?

    Kristen Doute's net worth is as big as her personality.

    Kristen's net worth is impressive at $1 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Kristen has many ventures on her plate. She has written a book called "He's Making You Crazy: How to Get the Guy, Get Even, and Get Over It." She's a co-founder of James Mae, which is a t-shirt company focused on graphic T's. She also has a podcast with her boyfriend called "Balancing Act," where they discuss everything from relationships to Bravo. Kristen has definitely kept busy since being let go from Vanderpump Rules.
