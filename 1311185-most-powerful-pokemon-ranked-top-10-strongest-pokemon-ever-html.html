    10 of the most powerful Pokemon

    Who is the strongest Pokemon of all time? Well, it may be difficult to know which Pokemon is the strongest because it depends on a number of things, including stats, types, moves, and abilities.     Ranking 10 on the list of the most powerful is the 'Original Dragon.' The original dragon was the only fusion of Zekrom, Kyurem, and Reshiram. The three original legendary parts are strong enough on their own, but when fused, their combined power brings their level to over 9000.

    9. Regigigas: The Continental Titan!
