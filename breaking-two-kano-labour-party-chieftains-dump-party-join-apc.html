    Two Kano Labour Party Chieftains Dump Party, Join APC

    Reports reaching Naija News at the moment confirmed that the state secretary of the party withdrew his membership and immediately pledged allegiance to the ruling All Progressives Congress (APC). Also, the LPs senatorial candidate for Kano central district, Malam Hamisu Magaji, is reportedly on his way to picking a membership card of the APC.

    02 Feb 2024, 00:00

    At least two Labour Party (LP) chieftains in Kano State have reportedly dumped the party ahead of the 2023 general elections.

    Reports reaching Naija News at the moment confirmed that the state secretary of the party withdrew his membership and immediately pledged allegiance to the ruling All Progressives Congress (APC).

    Also, the LP’s senatorial candidate for Kano central district, Malam Hamisu Magaji, is reportedly on his way to picking a membership card of the APC.

    President Muhammadu Buhari’s Special Assistant on Digital Communications, Bashir Ahmad made the disclosure on Wednesday, stating that Magaji was all set to join APC and work tirelessly for Asiwaju Bola Tinubu’s victory in the coming 2023 election.

    The lawmaker representing Egbeda/Ona Ara Federal Constituency of Oyo State, Akin Alabi on Wednesday warned supporters of the APC presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu to prepare to be bullied as elections kick off.

    Naija News reports that political campaigns will officially begin today, September 28 in line with the election timetable of the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC)

    The Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) presidential candidate, Atiku Abubakar is set to inaugurate his campaign team today while Peter Obi’s Labour Party (LP) and APC have postponed theirs indefinitely.

    Speaking, however, on the kick-off of the campaign season via Twitter, Alabi said that Tinubu is the most qualified Nigerian to lead Nigeria.

    He advised his fellow supporters to get ready to be insulted for their support but told them to stick to their decision and share it with others.
