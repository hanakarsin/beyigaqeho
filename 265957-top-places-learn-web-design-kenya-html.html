    Website design is a major concern in the Kenyan design industry. Creative website design is overly characterized by simplicity and responsiveness. Every design element, digital piece, and illustration in a website is expected to be clean and bold. Search engine optimization is another key consideration when it comes to commercial website designs. SEO enables clients to access website materials, eventually increasing the overall sales.

    Web design in Kenya has taken a new route in graphic design. Brands are now defined visually by captivating internet labels. Before choosing a suitable place to learn about web design, there are some factors to take into consideration. Cases in point are competitive analysis, academic quality, and competitive fee rates.

    There are several colleges offering web design in Kenya. Most of these colleges equip learners with creative graphics development ideas. It is desirable to pick on colleges with sufficient mastery of tutors in every aspect of web design. Skilled website designers are resourceful when sharpening on different design skills. Another important factor to consider when choosing a college to enroll for website design classes in programming, coding, and graphic design it a payable fee.

    Diploma in Web Design in Kenya

    The number of colleges and universities offering a diploma in website design or web development is continually increasing. The duration of this course varies across institutions with full time and part time offers. Below are a few of these institutions offering web design studies at diploma level;

    Institute of Advanced Technology

    IAT is based in Nairobi and offers a comprehensive program in website development. Learners interested in enrolling for this program must comply with minimum requirements as stipulated by the institution. Web design program at IAT takes up to 4 semesters of study. Interested learners should have at least one of the following qualifications;

    A minimum grade of C plain in Kenya certificate of secondary education

    Relevant qualification certificate from a recognized institution

    Or KCE division III

    Mombasa Airways Training Institute

    Mombasa Airways Training Institute is popularly known as MATI. The institution is located in Mombasa and offers exclusive website design programs. Some of the popular programs taught at MATI include HTML 5, JavaScript, Adobe Fireworks, PHP, Adobe Dreamweaver, and Adobe Illustrator. Website development course at MATI takes up to three months. After that, learners at MATI are examined by Mombasa Airways Training Institute, which is the main examination body.

    Kivuli College

    Kivuli College is found at Wangige and equips learners with high-end skills in HTML/CSS, SQL, JavaScript, and PHP programming languages. These programs take up to 3 months at a fee of KEs. 15,000. At the end of this program, learners at Kivuli are expected to have comprehended enough skills to develop their websites from scratch.

    Web Design Jobs Kenya

    Quality website designers are highly wanted and often land huge design contracts. Kenyan market is rapidly ripening on technology giving more hope to the industry. Website design is unlimited and offers a new opportunity for businesses and companies that take advantage of its benefits.

    Many attachments, internships, and jobs are rampantly being advertised on the internet. It is overwhelming that only a few of ICT practitioners have sufficient skills to apply for them. Most evident is the fact that not all ICT graduates have what it takes to develop, manage, and run huge traffic websites while monitoring large databases. For this reason, specialist web designers are urgently required to bridge the underlying market gap.

    Web design jobs in Kenya are mainly advertised through the star, Brighter Monday, career point, glass door and other popular job advertisement and placement companies in Kenya and outside Kenya.
