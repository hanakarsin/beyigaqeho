Buhari Vs Ortom: Why We Summoned Channels TV

17 Feb 2024, 00:00

The National Broadcasting Commission (NBC) has finally reacted to the query it issued to a popular broadcast station, Channels Television. The regulator also summoned two of the station’s presenters, Chamberlain Usoh and Kayode Okikiolu, to Abuja over the interview. Speaking on the reason the commission queried the media outlet, NBC Director-General, Balarabe Ilelah, said the media outlet failed to adhere to its regulations during the interview.
