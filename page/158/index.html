Umahi Speaks On Accusing Obiano, Soludo Of Rigging Anambra Election

08 Mar 2024, 00:00

Governor Dave Umahi of Ebonyi State has denied accusing his Anambra counterpart, Willie Obiano and the governorship candidate of the All Progressives Grand Alliance (APGA), Charles Soludo of rigging the election. In a statement on Monday signed by his spokesman, Nwaze Francis, the governor refuted reports linking to him to a comment that the Anambra election was marred with irregularities. Umahi, however, advised the public to disregard the fake and baseless news, adding that he never made such remarks about the Anambra gubernatorial election.
