    Chidzuga praises ailing daughter

    The proud mother of four, who had kept her young child's predicament private for long, told the world how proud she was of her three-year-old daughter during the little one’s birthday in 2020.

    The scribe was proud of the angel for holding on and keeping a brave face as life threw her one challenge after the other.

    Chidzuga's daughter turns 18

    A couple of months ago, the former TV girl celebrated her firstborn daughter after turning 18 years old.

    The riveting former K24 Swahili news anchor described her 18-year-old daughter as a child filled with joy that spills to the entire family.

    "To my dearest daughter Hafsah You just turned 18 years old today. I thank Allah for giving us the greatest blessing of our lives. And for you calling me mum. Looking back at the 17 years what a journey it has been full of blessings laughter joy love tears and the list goes on. A new chapter of your life starts today," part of her message read.
