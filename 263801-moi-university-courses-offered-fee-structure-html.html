    Moi University offers a diverse set of courses tailored for different students who wish to pursue the courses of their dreams. The institution caters for students from different backgrounds and is among the most populous in the region. Before applying to the University, it’s important to find out any information regarding the course that you find suited for your dream career and goals. This post offers a list of courses offered at Moi University.

    Moi University fee structure

    As a student, you might want to consider joining any university that has the best courses with reasonable fees. Moi University, surprisingly, charges fair fees too. Take an example, in the school of business, a government sponsored student, first year first semester pays Ksh. 14,550 and in the second semester pays Ksh. 8,100. In total, you will pay Kshs. 22,650. This clearly shows how awesome this university fee is.

    For a more detailed list of the fees for each and every course they offer, visit their website at https://www.mu.ac.ke/.
