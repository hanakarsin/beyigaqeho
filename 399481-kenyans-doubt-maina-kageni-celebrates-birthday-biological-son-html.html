    Popular radio presenter Maina Kageni has all along been known to be single.

    As a matter of fact, many have for years prevailed upon him to get married and start a family given that he is not only a man of means but also possesses a charisma that can get him many suitors.

    The Maina and King'ang'i in the Morning presenter remains one of the highest paid radio voices in Kenya but has no known love partner or child.

    Read also

    It, therefore, came as a surprise when he uploaded a photo of himself and a young boy on social media, ostensibly in celebration of his biological son's birthday.

    Speaking to a fellow journalist at Classic FM, Maina revealed that the four-year-old is called Nathan.

    He added that his existence has remained a top secret because he saw no need to expose the child to the limelight given he is a public figure himself.

    “Nathan lives a very good life, surrounded by love,” he said.

    Having been raised by a single mother after his father's death when he was only two years old, speculations have been rife that the king of radio may be skeptical about his abilities to be a good father.

    On the 2013 Fathers' Day, Maina celebrated his mother for having played the role of both parents and nurturing him to greatness.

    "I never knew my father… he passed when I was barely out of my diapers," he wrote.

    His birthday post attracted a host of comments as Kenyans concluded it is one of his stunts, while the curious ones wanted to know details like who the mother of the four-year-old boy was.

    He, however, refused to divulge details of the child's mother adding:

    "That’s my family. You know I don’t belong to you people like the Royal family. You don’t have to know about her,” he maintained.

    It will be remembered that the 46-year-old has always had a heart for philanthropy, having 'adopted' another young man called Anthony Muchori and educating him all the way to university.

    The announcement that he has a biological son, if true, will change the perception on many people's minds and add yet another feather to his well-decorated cap of a presenter, MC, philanthropist, and social media influencer.
