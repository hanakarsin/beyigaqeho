    The cast of 'Jewish Matchmaking' is on Instagram. Read on for the details of all the potential catches and where to find them on social media.

    Fans of Indian Matchmaking rejoice! On May 3, 2023, Netflix introduced a new series about finding love for singles with the chutzpah to believe they are ready to find the one.

    Jewish Matchmaking "features singles in the U.S. and Israel as they turn their dating life over to a top Jewish matchmaker," per the show's description.

    Article continues below advertisement

    Indeed, this reality dating series is for Jewish contenders who hope to head to the chuppah to say "I do" in the very near future. With the help of professional matchmaker Aleeza Ben Shalom, we sure hope they find love!

    In the meantime, let's keep up with the cast of Jewish Matchmaking on their Instagrams.

    Let's meet the cast of 'Jewish Matchmaking'.

    Aleeza is the Sima Taparia of Jewish Matchmaking.

    She has fixed up hundreds of couples and hopes to match more on Season 1 of the buzzy Netflix show. She shares content on Instagram using the handle @aleezabenshalom.
