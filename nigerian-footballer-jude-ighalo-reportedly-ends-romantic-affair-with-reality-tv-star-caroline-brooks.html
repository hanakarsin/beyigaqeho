    12 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Nigerian footballer, Jude Ighalo and Dubia reality TV star, Caroline Brooks, are in the news over the crash of their romantic relationship.

    Naija News reports that the latest development was made known by Nigerian celebrity journalist, Stella Dimoko Korkus, stating that Caroline is presently single again and with a son.

    According to her, the Real Housewives of Dubai star reportedly ended her steamy love affair with Ighalo, and close associates of the pair are whispering about the multiple reasons the affair ended.

    Korkus said the romantic affair ended over serial lies and cheating, and Brooks is reportedly telling friends that the relationship became too toxic to continue.

    Neither Caroline nor Jude has ever publicly posted about their breakup. However, Stella alleged that the good-looking pair split about six months ago.

    She also alleged that close friends of the love birds claimed the pair met and started their hot affair while Jude was separated from his beautiful ex-wife Sonia Adesua, and he is currently single as their divorce pulled through in 2022.

    Stella noted that friends of Caroline are happy she is single again so that she can find a good guy to be with, adding that the lies and cheating in the relationship became so much that she had to constantly pinch herself back to reality.
