    Kenyan vlogger donates kidney to his dad, grateful surgery was successful

    Kenyan vlogger Rowney Insane has donated one of his kidneys to his father and has thanked his fans for prayers after a successful surgery.

    The vlogger shared a lengthy post on his Instagram page, thanking his fans for their encouragement and prayers even as he went for surgery.

    He shared that he donated one of his kidneys to his father who was sick and that he thanked God that both of them were doing fine.

    "This is a story that I haven't shared for a while now, but having consulted my parents about it, we saw it's the best thing as someone may be going through the same situation and hope this encourages someone,"

    ''We had kidney transplant on the 10th of January 2021 which was a success and we can't be grateful enough for God's might and strength and people who have been with us in this journey especially those who have been praying for us and who came through when we had a fund raising. I being the donor, it has not been easy but having you guys motivates me and being there when I needed you guys, you never failed and now I have a reason to smile as my dad is okay now," he said.

    Insane added his dad was responding very well and that he could "not hide his joy and now he has a story to testify" following the transplant "that indeed kidney transplant works and it can be done here in Kenya and be successful".
